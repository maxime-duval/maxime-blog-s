Match analysis

UBB vs Lyon

In this last match at Chaban Delmas between UBB and Lyon, a fierce battle took place. Indeed, despite being away from home, Lyon took the lead immediately, scoring in the seventh minute. It was a chilling moment for the whole stadium. However, UBB bounced back, with Matthieu Jalibert scoring three penalties to put Bordeaux ahead at half-time. After the break, it was a physical battle with lots of contact. And on 65 minutes, Baptiste Couilloud, the young scrum-half, scored a final try for Lyon. Only ten minutes remained for Bordeaux to regain the lead. Thanks to the entire stadium, the Bordeaux team rallied and came back into the game with a last-ditch effort. And it was finally Bordeaux center Moefana who scored to set the stadium free. A great performance from Lyon.
